Our Company

Our environment

The Drink House offers a unique and antique coffee house environment unlike any other in Malaysia.


Our place is suitable for you to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee, tea and juice.


I f you need to work, we have a seating area created specially for you.


If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in an indoor garden which perfect for your weary mind and body.

Our Coffee

We are using Arabica and Robusta coffee beans as the main ingredients of our coffee.


Arabica coffee beans tend to showcase a softer, sweeter taste, richly infused with flavour notes of fruit, sugar and berries while Robusta beans have a harsher, bitter taste and a less saturated aroma.


A blend of 80% Arabica and 20% Robusta make our coffee even more delicious.


Our local baristas know that great espresso takes more than pushing a button. We work hard to deliver great taste while also providing knowledgeable service, perfect if you just want to try something new.
